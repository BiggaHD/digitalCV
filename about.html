About Me

A brief insight into the complex, but pleasant person that makes me

Little Intro:

I'm highly obsessed when it comes to learning all sort of things. That's what keeps me going in life. These days I'm most passionate about CS. Coding is a new challenge for me and I won't slow down until I feel like I achieved some level of mastery. Then the actual fun begins. Until that moment, it's all about self-discipline.

My life philosophy/ personality goes something like this:

Don't tell me I can't do it; Don't tell me it can't be done.
I like to collaborate, to share knowledge and positive vibes. I'm a true team player. But what can affect me in a negative way is someone claiming I can't do something, especially work-related. Okay, maybe he/ she is right about the first part, but if I can't do it by myself, then WE can absolutely achieve it together. I stand corrected, so please just don't share the rumor that something is undoable. Let's stay calm, focused, driven, and by the end of the day let's make this work.

My desire is to be a part of it, a small part of that team. The winning team. My goal is to contribute to the resolution that will be the solution to the situation we are all in today and which differs significantly from the one in which we were in previous years (still to this day). I'm craving to be a small part of the change.
